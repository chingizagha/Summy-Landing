Privacy Policy for Summy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Summy! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the App includes:

Personal Data

  • Email address (for account creation and support)
  • Username or display name
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Text summarization history and preferences
  • Subscription and payment information (processed securely through Apple's App Store)

Usage Data

  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Text processing statistics and performance data
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • Log data and analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our text summarization app
  • Process your monthly or annual subscription through Apple's App Store
  • Store your summarization history and preferences
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Improve our app and develop new features
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

4. Subscription Information

Summy offers premium subscriptions with two options:

  • Monthly Subscription: Access to unlimited text summarization and premium features
  • Annual Subscription: Full year access with cost savings

All subscription payments are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not store your payment information directly. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except as described in this policy:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our app
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: Information may be transferred if we undergo a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Summarization history and preferences are retained to provide you with a continuous experience across app sessions.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Cancel your subscription through the App Store

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
